Welcome

MDM Specialist Trades has over 30 years of experience in the construction industry, having been initially established in 1981.
Originally trading as Sweeney Drywall, we re-branded in 2006 following an MBO and now trade as MDM Specialist Trades Ltd.
More recently, in 2017, the company changed hands once again and is now run by 2 directors: Stephen Watters and Scott Thompson
Our directors pride themselves in producing quality, reliability and service to the construction and new build housing industries.
Our business activities encompass all markets from new build housing and social housing projects to commercial projects.
